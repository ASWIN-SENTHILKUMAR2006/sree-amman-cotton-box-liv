Cotton Sourcing & Quality Assurance

From farm to factory — how we ensure every box meets the highest quality standards through rigorous sourcing and testing.

Our Sourcing Pillars

Six core practices that define our quality commitment

🤝

Direct Farmer Procurement

We work directly with over 200 cotton farmers across Tamil Nadu, eliminating intermediaries. This ensures fair pricing for farmers and premium raw material for our boxes. Each farmer is verified and trained in quality harvesting practices.

⭐

Grade A Cotton Selection

Only the top-grade cotton fibers make it to production. Our quality team inspects each batch for fiber length, strength, and cleanliness. We reject substandard material at source, maintaining a 98.5% quality approval rate.

Moisture Control Storage

Cotton is stored in climate-controlled warehouses with precise humidity regulation. Our storage maintains 8-10% moisture content, preventing fiber degradation and ensuring optimal processing characteristics for corrugation.

🔬

Fiber Density Testing

Every cotton batch undergoes laboratory testing for fiber density, tensile strength, and cellulose content. We use TAPPI-standard testing equipment to ensure consistent board quality in every production run.

Sustainable Sourcing

We are committed to environmentally responsible sourcing. Our partner farmers use organic farming methods and water-efficient irrigation. We track our supply chain carbon footprint and offset through tree-planting programs.

In-House Cotton Processing

Our vertically integrated facility processes raw cotton into corrugated board in-house. This gives us full control over pulping, sheet formation, and corrugation — ensuring the highest consistency and quality at every stage.
