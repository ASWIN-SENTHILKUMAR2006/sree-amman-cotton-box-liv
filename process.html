Our Manufacturing Process

From raw cotton to finished box — 7 precision-controlled steps ensure unmatched quality in every product we deliver.

1

Raw Cotton Collection

Grade A cotton is collected directly from our partner farmers across Tamil Nadu. Each batch is weighed, catalogued, and inspected for fiber quality before transport to our facility. We maintain full traceability from farm to factory.

2

🧹 Cleaning & Sorting

Raw cotton undergoes thorough cleaning to remove impurities, dust, and short fibers. Mechanical and air-based cleaning systems sort fibers by length and quality grade. Only premium fibers proceed to pulping.

3

📄 Corrugation Layer Formation

Clean cotton fibers are pulped and formed into flat sheets. The corrugation machine then flutes the medium layer into a wave pattern and bonds it between liner sheets using high-strength adhesive, creating the signature corrugated structure.

4

📦 Box Structuring

Corrugated sheets are precisely cut, scored, and folded into box shapes using CNC die-cutting machines. Slots, flaps, and hand-hold openings are created based on customer specifications. Custom printing is applied at this stage.

5

⚙️ Compression Testing

Every production batch undergoes box compression testing (BCT) to verify stacking strength. We test edge crush resistance (ECT)+, burst strength, and flat crush to ensure each box meets or exceeds the rated load capacity.

6

✅ Quality Inspection

A dedicated QA team performs visual and dimensional inspection on random samples from each run. Boxes are checked for print alignment, color accuracy, fold precision, adhesive bonding, and overall finish. Defective units are rejected.

7

🚛 Packaging & Dispatch

Approved boxes are bundled, stretch-wrapped, and palletized for safe transit. Orders are dispatched with full documentation including quality certificates, test reports, and delivery tracking. Pan-India delivery within 5-7 business days.
